Welcome to Tabless Thursday!

This tool will provide an easy-to-use interface to manage your browser and prioritize your work

Increase Productivity

We all know someone how always has way too many tabs open on their webpages. Let's be honest, many of us are those people. With Tabless Thursday, you can eliminate the clutter that tabs create and allow you to focus on the most important projects.

Turn Tabs Into To-Do's

Tabless Thursday will not only allow you to remove the clutter at the top of your webpage, but it will turn these into an organized list of "to-do's" that you can prioritize to help you know which items are of higher priority.
